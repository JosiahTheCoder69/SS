Social Studies - Unit 1: Connecting Themes

Major Themes of Social Studies

In this unit, we introduce the key themes that will guide our understanding of social studies, such as conflict, culture, governance, and human-environment interaction.

Conflict and Change

Conflict within or between societies often leads to change. This can be seen throughout history as societies evolve due to wars, social movements, or political disputes.

Culture

Culture is shaped by religion, beliefs, customs, traditions, and government, and it varies across different societies.
