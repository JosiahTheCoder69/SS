Georgia Standards of Excellence - 6th Grade Social Studies

Geography Standards

  • SS6G7: Locate selected features of Europe on a world and regional map.
  • SS6G8: Explain the impact of location, climate, and natural resources on trade and population distribution in Europe.
  • SS6G9: Discuss the environmental issues in Europe, including acid rain in Germany, air pollution in the United Kingdom, and the Chernobyl nuclear disaster.

History Standards

  • SS6H3: Explain the impact of World War I and the Great Depression on Europe.
  • SS6H4: Explain the rise of Nazism, the Holocaust, and the events of World War II in Europe.
  • SS6H5: Discuss the impact of the Cold War in Europe, including the fall of the Berlin Wall and the breakup of the Soviet Union.

Economics Standards

  • SS6E5: Explain how voluntary trade benefits buyers and sellers in Europe.
  • SS6E6: Describe factors that influence economic growth and examine the role of human capital, capital goods, natural resources, and entrepreneurship in Europe.

Government and Civics Standards

  • SS6CG3: Compare the various forms of government in Europe, including democracy, autocracy, and oligarchy.
  • SS6CG4: Explain the structure of modern European governments, including the parliamentary system in the United Kingdom and the federal system in Germany.
