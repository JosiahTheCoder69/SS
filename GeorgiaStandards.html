Georgia Standards of Excellence - 6th Grade Social Studies

Geography Standards

  • Locate geographical features of Latin America, Canada, Europe, and Australia on maps.
  • Explain how geography impacts population, trade, and the environment.

Environmental Standards

  • Explain the causes and effects of acid rain in Germany, air pollution in the UK, and the Chernobyl disaster.
