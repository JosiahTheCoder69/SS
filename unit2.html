Unit 2: Geography of Europe

Key Geographical Features of Europe

  • Danube River: A major waterway in Central and Eastern Europe.
  • Rhine River: One of the most important rivers in Western Europe.
  • European Plain: A vast stretch of flat land that supports agriculture and population.
  • The Alps: A major mountain range providing natural borders and climate influences.
  • Pyrenees Mountains: A mountain range between France and Spain.

Environmental Issues in Europe

  • Acid Rain in Germany, caused by industrial pollution.
  • Air Pollution in the United Kingdom due to industrialization.
  • The Chernobyl Nuclear Disaster in Ukraine.

Georgia Standards for Unit 2

  • SS6G7: Locate selected features of Europe on a world and regional map.
  • SS6G8: Explain environmental issues in Europe.
  • SS6G9: Explain how location, climate, and natural resources affect trade and settlement.
