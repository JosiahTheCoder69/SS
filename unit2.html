Social Studies - Unit 2: Geography of Europe

Key Features of Europe

This unit focuses on major European geographical features like the Danube River, the Alps, and the Pyrenees.

  • Danube River - Central Europe's key waterway
  • The Alps - Major mountain range affecting Europe's climate
  • European Plain - A large expanse that impacts population and economy

Environmental Issues

We explore critical environmental challenges in Europe, including:

  • Acid Rain in Germany - Caused by industrial pollution
  • Air Pollution in the UK - Resulting from heavy industrialization
  • Nuclear Disaster in Chernobyl, Ukraine
