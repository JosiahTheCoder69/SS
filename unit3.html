Social Studies - Unit 3: World War I and the Great Depression

The Treaty of Versailles

After World War I, the Treaty of Versailles placed harsh reparations on Germany, leading to economic depression and setting the stage for further conflict.

The Global Depression

The Great Depression affected not only the United States but economies around the world, contributing to widespread unemployment and economic hardship.
