Flashcards - Social Studies

What was the primary cause of World War I?
The assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand.
What is the Treaty of Versailles?
A peace treaty that ended World War I and imposed harsh reparations on Germany.
What was a major result of the Great Depression?
Widespread unemployment and economic hardship across the globe.
What environmental issue affects Germany?
Acid rain caused by industrial pollution.
What major river runs through central Europe?
The Danube River.
